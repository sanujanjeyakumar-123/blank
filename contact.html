Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the calorie calculations?

+

Our calculations use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, which is considered one of the most accurate methods for calculating BMR. However, individual metabolism can vary, so use these as guidelines.

Are the recipes suitable for special diets?

+

Many of our recipes can be adapted for various dietary needs. We include nutritional information to help you make informed choices. Always consult with a healthcare provider for specific dietary requirements.

Can I save my workout plans?

+

Currently, workout plans are generated fresh each time. We're working on adding a feature to save and customize your favorite workouts in future updates.

Is my data stored securely?

+

All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We don't collect or store personal information on our servers, ensuring your privacy and security.
